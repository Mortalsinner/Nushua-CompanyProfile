About us

PT NASHUA NUSANTARA.

PT Nashua Nusantara, berbasis di Jakarta dan didirikan pada tahun 2006, adalah perusahaan yang mengkhususkan diri dalam produk pita perekat yang dikenal karena keawetan, keandalan, daya rekat kuat, ketahanan terhadap suhu ekstrem, dan kinerja tahan lama, dengan menyediakan baik produk standar maupun solusi kustom sesuai kebutuhan pelanggan.

PT Nashua Nusantara is a company based in Jakarta, Indonesia, specializing in adhesive tapes and related products. These tapes are recognized for their durability and reliability. Established in 2006, the company has a long-standing reputation for providing a wide range of adhesive tapes and other supporting materials. They cater to customer needs with both standard products and customized solutions tailored to specific requirements​. The brand is known for its strong adhesion, resistance to extreme temperatures, and long-lasting performance.
